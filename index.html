What is FujinKit?

FujinKit is a UI components library based on Web Components. Every UI library out there aims to be elegant, pixel-perfect, and full of life.

We do not.

We exist to serve the SASS (the traditional kind) builders of the world. Our mission? Create boring, lifeless, but highly usable components.

Every piece of our design is inspired by the beautiful bleakness of Indian government websites. Their soul-crushing, joyless layouts are our blueprint.

But even we, as users, get frustrated when those same sites are unusable or non-responsive. So, we took matters into our own hands.

FujinKit is open source and made with love (and a touch of despair), in the hope that one day â€” just maybe â€” a government agency will use our work and make something usable.

In democracy we trust!

Usage

You can simply add the script tag before closing the body tag we are ready to rock! 


        <!DOCTYPE html>
        <html lang="en">
          <head>
            <meta charset="UTF-8" />
            <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0" />
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="styles.css" />
            <title>Document</title>
          </head>
          <body>
            ...
            <script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/inomag/elements-prototype@v0.0.1-alpha/dist/fujin-kit-lib.min.js"></script>
          </body>
        </html>
